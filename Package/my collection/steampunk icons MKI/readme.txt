This folder has all my icons in .ICO format. Many are multiple size icons that
contain all the icons from 16x16 to 256x256. These are approx. 360k in size.
The small size icons are my older creations and may just be one size. There may 
be PNG versions of these in the other folders.

You may use any of my own images in your own creations but commercially only with 
my permission.

In all other non-commercial cases I require a credit to the original artist (me)
using my name and including a prominent link to my site(s).
